The Satpayev Medal 2024 (Almaty, Kazakhstan)

In August 2024, Dr. Maxat Kassen received the Kanysh Satpayev Medal from the National Academy of Sciences (Almaty, Kazakhstan). The prestigious award is given for significant scientific achievements and is dedicated to the 125th year anniversary of the academician.
